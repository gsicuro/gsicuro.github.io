    Istituzioni di Matematica 2 #

    Modulo per il corso di laurea triennale in Architettura–Ingegneria

    La pagina contiene del materiale per il modulo di Istituzioni di Matematica 2 del corso di laurea in Architettura-Ingegneria dell’Università di Bologna, tenuto durante il primo semestre l’anno accademico 2023-2024. Il corso consisteva di due parti. La prima si focalizzava su elementi dell’algebra lineare, mentre la seconda trattava i fondamenti del calcolo in molte variabili. In seguito è riportato l’elenco degli esami assegnati durante il periodo.

    Stochastic processes and applications #

    A module for the Master in Complex Systems Modelling at King’s College London

    Formerly known as Dynamical analysis of complex systems, I taught the module from 2021 to 2023. A set of lecture notes, largely based on a previous version prepared by A. Annibale, was provided to the students. The module was part of the KCL Complex Systems Modelling master.

    Foundation of complex systems modelling #

    A module for the Master in Complex Systems Modelling at King’s College London

    The module was intended as a primer in essential mathematical tools for complex systems. I taught the module from 2021 to 2023. A set of lecture notes, largely based on a previous version prepared by A. Annibale, was provided to the students. The module was part of the KCL Complex Systems Modelling master.
